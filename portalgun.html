Portal Gun

About the portal gun

Aperture Science Handheld Portal Device also known as portal gun long for ASHPD The ASHPD has the ability to manufacture two linked portals at once a blue portal will be manufactured when hitting the first trigger and an orange one hitting the second trigger When entering a blue portal one will always come out an orange portal and vice versa. The ASHPD is held with a hand behind the weapon, holding the handle/trigger. The other hand is placed under the cannon. A bar in its glass tube has the color of the last shot portal.
